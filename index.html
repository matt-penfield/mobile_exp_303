Songwriting Tips
Try This

Write in F major at ~120 BPM using only two chords (F and G). The power of this song is its simplicity — the hypnotic loop creates space for melody and lyrics to shine.

Arrangement Tip

Keep your arrangement nearly identical across verses and choruses. Differentiate sections through vocal melody and lyric intensity rather than instrumental changes.

Instrumentation Tip

Layer a fingerpicked electric guitar pattern over a steady kick-snare groove. Add keyboards for warmth but keep them low in the mix — texture, not melody.

Mood Tip

To capture the dreamy, floating quality: use Mixolydian mode (major scale with ♭7), keep the tempo moderate, and let vocals carry reverb with a slight delay.
